Pros and cons of social media

Pros:

  • Great marketing tools
  • Online learning tools
  • Entertainment
  • Quick access to information and research
  • Staying connected with friends and family worldwide via email, text, FaceTime, etc...

    • Cons:

  • Used too much. The more time people spend on social media, the bigger chance it will lead to cyberbullying, depression, social anxiety, etc...
  • Fear of missing out. FOMO has become very common and often will lead to continually checking social media. The idea that you might miss out on something if you are not online can affect your mental health.

    • What you can do

    Try putting your phone down 1 hour before you go to bed, this can help you sleep better, and you won't wake up tired that can help with education! You should also take breaks so you aren't staring at your device for hours.
